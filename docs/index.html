Proposed CAB Meeting Times

17 January, 2025

Introduction

This contains a set of proposals for possible CAB meetings times to be voted on. The full explanations are here.

Meeting times for members are classified as:

  1. Business Hours: 8am to 5pm meeting times
  2. Outside Hours: 6-7am or 6-10pm meeting times
  3. Horrible: 11pm to 5am meeting times

These can be considered overall as a reasonable description, despite some members finding a midnight meeting as quite convenient and a 6am start to be horrifying, with others being completely the opposite.

Current Schedule

This schedule was devised by shifting the meeting in 8 hour increments every 4 months.

Fixed At 9am US/Eastern

This proposal attempts to help Lori continually attend, as member of the Bioconductor Core Team and CAB secretary, by starting at 9am US/Eastern every month. For some of the year, this potentially enables full attendance.

Visualisation

Summary Table

Summary of meeting times for all members under the schedule fixing start times at 9am US/Eastern. Members in the Asia/Pacific region are the most strongly disadvantaged.

Setting Start Times By The Western-Most Members

This proposal shifts times every 4 months placing the western-most member of each region as the earliest start time (6am). Until March, US/Eastern was chosen instead of Europe/Dublin as an attempt to slightly maximise attendance, given most US members find meetings after 10pm to be difficult to attend.

Difficult Meeting Times in Empty Time-Zones

This proposal attempts to place meetings which might start between 1 and 5am either in the Pacific, between Australia & the US, or between Eastern Europe and Western Asia.
